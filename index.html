4. Absorbed Dose to Water Determination

Global corrections (kTP, kpol, ks, kelec) are applied to Mraw automatically.

Mcorr = Avg Mraw × kTP × kelec × kpol × ks

Dw(zmax) [SSD] = ((Mcorr × ND,w × kQ) / MU / PDD) × 100

Dw(zmax) [SAD] = ((Mcorr × ND,w × kQ) / MU) / TPR

Variation = ((Measured - Ref Output) / Ref Output) × 100
